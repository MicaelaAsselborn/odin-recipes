Crepes (The argentinean style)

Description

This is a basic recipe for crepes, but, because I'm from Argentina, I'm going to give you the argentinean version! You can use it for any crepe recipe you want.

Ingredients

  • Flour: 200gr
  • Milk: 500ml
  • Eggs: 2
  • Salt: A pinch
  • Butter: 50gr

Steps

  1. Put the flour in a bowl
  2. Add the milk and mix it well
  3. Add the eggs and mix it well
  4. Add the salt and mix it well
  5. Put the butter in a pan and heat it
  6. Put a little bit of the dough in the pan and spread it
  7. Cook it for 1 minute
  8. Flip it and cook it for 30 seconds
  9. Repeat until you finish the dough

And done! They are ready to eat with ham and cheese, or dulce de leche, or any filling of your liking really!
