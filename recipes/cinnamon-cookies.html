Cinnamon cookies

Description

I came up with this recipe when I was staying on a relative house on France. I was jobless, bored and hungry, so I made this recipe following a recipe book, but tweaking it a little since i didn't had all the ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Butter: 125gr
  • Powdered cinnamon: A tea spoon
  • Sugar: 150gr
  • Flour: 200gr
  • Eggs: 1

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180ÂºC
  2. Mix the butter with the sugar until it's creamy
  3. Add the egg and mix it well
  4. Add the flour and the cinnamon and mix it well
  5. Make small balls with the dough and put them on a baking tray
  6. Bake for 10 - 15 minutes

Voila! Enjoy your cookies!
